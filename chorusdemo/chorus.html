About


Chorus is Sharda University's biggest annual Cultural Festival and has established itself as a legacy where everyone looks forward to coming together be it, competing or enjoying. Offering a rich blend of music, Art, dance, theatrics and numerous other fun events, Chorus promises an exuberant garland of cultural diversity, learning and never-ending entertainment. Continuing with the tradition of providing an academically and culturally stimulating atmosphere, we hope to build on the grand success of the previous Chorus and make this one more magnificent.
